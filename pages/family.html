The Kids

Jack

Jack is the Superhero, always seeking to make things right, and he doesn’t forget to have fun doing it! He goes silly writing his own puns and jokes and keeps the camera rolling like a pro. With a heart for justice, he can connect with anyone on a mission to do good in the world.

About Me: “I try to be just plain awesome. I love justice, origami and bizarre trivia. I’m a nerdy athlete and a stop-motion freak.”

About the Road Trip:  “I want to hang loose, not worry, and inspire people to be awesome through our project.”

Solveig

Solveig is the Dreamer. Her name means "sun ray" and she's full of light and life! With passion, she throws herself into things fully. She doesn’t forget her dreams and daily adds new items to her bucket list. As an includer, she cares deeply about the hopes of others, too.

About Me: “I’m excited about friends and always want to have a date with my mom or dad. I’m musical and love to sing and act silly.”

About the Road Trip: “I want to learn how I can change the world.”

Evie

 

Evie is our little Fighter. She seeks to conquer, is super determined, and does not give up easily. She’s also the baby of the family, so at home she is still the snuggly, thumb-sucking little girl you can’t resist.

About Me: “I’m a mamma-snuggler and love playing with my friends. I love practicing handstands and reading with my family.”

About the Road Trip: “I want to find out what kids my age can do to help make the world better.”

The Parents

Eva

Eva is the Compassionate Warrior of our family. She takes the world head on with empathy for others and isn’t afraid of being exposed to all things new.  She loves nature, learning, and soaking up deep spiritual lessons from her experiences. Her favorite time of day is family reading time on the sofa.

About Me: “I love people, meaningful conversations, new challenges, my family and acting crazy together.”

About the Road Trip: “My hope is for my kids to realize that they can make a significant difference, and to learn from the parents we’ll be meeting about how to best parent a change-maker. “

Matt

Matt is the Storyteller and loves how stories have the power to change people and move them to action. As a collaborator, his primary goal is to create an environment of respect and care for those with whom he creates. He also loves cooking, reading stories to his family, and snuggling on the sofa watching movies.

About Me: “I love telling and reading stories to my kids. I can’t get enough of their wonder and amazement. I love learning from them.”

About the Road Trip: “More than anything, I hope that our kids and those around the country will be inspired to go be awesome themselves.”
