The Team

Dawn LaMattina Asher (Producer)

Dawn got her start in the entertainment trailer industry as a copywriter and producer of major studio film, television and home video advertising. She is now primarily in the business of documentary filmmaking and is the Founder & Executive Producer of her own creative production company, Tribe of Asher. Dawn runs an online journal Good Taste & Gatherings, where good people make good food even better. Dawn is a true Foodie by Italian descent.


Dave Torstenson (Producer)

Dave is no stranger to roadtrip documentary adventures: he created, produced, and stars in Circle the Wagen, a buddy roadtrip "docu-dramedy" about a cross-country trip in a 1972 Volkswagen Bus. Having earned his television production stripes at Good Morning America and MTV News, Dave has since traveled to conflict and post-conflict regions around the world to teach documentary filmmaking through the Tiziano Project. Dave is currently based out of Los Angeles and builds web solutions for the Customer Experience team at Hulu, crafts social media strategies for nonprofits and startup companies, and moonlights as an accordionist whenever he gets a moment to breathe.
